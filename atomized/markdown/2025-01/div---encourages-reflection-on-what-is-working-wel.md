<div>   - Encourages reflection on what is working well and what could be improved.  </div>
<div>   - Helps the instructor adjust the course to better fit student needs.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>2. **Prompt Options**  </div>
<div>   - **Concrete:** What aspects of this course have been most helpful to you so far? What would you change?  </div>
<div>   - **Abstract:** What does a supportive learning environment look like to you? How can a course structure help or hinder learning?  </div>
<div>   - **Connecting:** Think of a time when you struggled with an assignment or concept in this course. What helped you overcome the challenge?  </div>
<div>   - **Exploratory:** If you were designing this course, what would you do differently?  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>3. **Discussion After Writing**  </div>
<div>   - What feedback do you think is most valuable for an instructor to receive?  </div>
<div>   - How can student input shape the way a course is taught?  </div>
<div>   - Did writing about your experience change how you view your role as a student?  </div>
