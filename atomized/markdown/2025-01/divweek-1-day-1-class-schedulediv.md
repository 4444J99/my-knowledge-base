<div>I. Writing Warm-Up (15 minutes)</div>
<div>Purpose</div>
<div>    •  A daily exercise to ease into writing, develop fluency, and build confidence in expressing thoughts.</div>
<div>  Prompt Options</div>
<div>      •  Concrete: Describe a moment in your life when clear communication made a significant difference. What was the situation, and how did effective communication impact the outcome?</div>
<div>      •  Abstract: What does “voice” mean to you beyond just speaking? How do you think your personal voice influences how others perceive you?</div>
<div>      •  Connecting: Consider how your written voice differs from your spoken voice. In what ways do you think writing allows you to communicate differently or more effectively than speaking?</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Discussion After Writing (5 minutes)</div>
<div>    •  What challenges did you face in writing without stopping?</div>
<div>    •  How does freewriting compare to your usual writing habits?</div>
<div>    •  Did your thoughts flow easily, or did you struggle to get started?</div>
