<div>### **III. Lecture: The Art of Narrative Writing**  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>1. **What Makes a Strong Narrative?**  </div>
<div>   - Clear structure, engaging details, and a compelling voice.  </div>
<div>   - Strong narratives evoke emotion, create tension, and provide resolution.  </div>
<div>   - The principle of “show, don’t tell” enhances reader engagement.  </div>
<div><br></div>

<div>   - **Connecting:** How does storytelling influence your perception of the world?  </div>
