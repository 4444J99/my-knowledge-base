<div>IV. Assignment Overview: Narrative Essay Prewriting (Introduction Only) (30 minutes)</div>
<div>  1.  Purpose of the Narrative Essay</div>
<div>  •  Students will craft a personal story that conveys a lesson learned or an important moment.</div>
<div>  2.  Key Components</div>
<div>  •  Selecting a meaningful personal story.</div>
<div>  •  Developing a clear beginning, middle, and end.</div>
<div>  •  Incorporating vivid descriptions and dialogue.</div>
<div>  3.  Prewriting Strategies</div>
<div>  •  Freewriting: Set a timer for 5-10 minutes and write nonstop.</div>
<div>  •  Listing: Brainstorm key events, emotions, and themes.</div>
<div>  •  Mind Mapping: Visually organize characters, themes, and events.</div>
<div>  •  Questioning: Ask key questions to develop ideas.</div>
<div>  •  Outlining: Plan the beginning, middle, and end before drafting.</div>
