<div>Here’s the problem, no one can talk about themselves accurately. And no one can present a full idea of total breath and width of a person’s entirety (their body, spirit, and capabilities) through words or a stack of resumes. Resumes only paint half a picture of a full person. So while acknowledging this impossibility, it then only makes sense to absurdly proceed forward foolheartedly. The fool is the beginning of the Tarot, the 0 card, so with this then we can step over the ledge into a new field. In this sense then being a beginner is where creativity thrives (with control and understanding of the technical aspect). Lou Reed said he never practiced a day in his life, so only picked up a guitar when he was going to write a song. Picasso said to steal boldly. </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Here’s what I believe: anyone can be president or ceo of wherever or whatever. We’re either in a process of blooming or decaying. </div>
<div><br></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Narrative is the thing that has always made most sense to me. It is the lens through which we can experience universal cathartic experiences and cleanse ourselves of life’s burdens. A tradition I intend to keep with my progeny, was my mom read to me in bed every night. The Lion the witch and The Wardrobe. Since then I’ve dedicated my life to writing and to understanding the power of the written word. Everything (be it a film, advertisement, or a song) starts with emotion and emotion is expressed in narrative and is expressed in the written word. I think therefore I am! We master our thoughts through the rendering of words. </div>
