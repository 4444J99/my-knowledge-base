<div><b><tt>I. Lawyer Call Script – 3 Versions (for Calls, Voicemails, or Emails)</tt></b></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><b><tt>1. Short (Elevator Style)</tt></b><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>Hi, I’m seeking a probate attorney in Broward County. My partner recently passed and I’m dealing with estate fraud, funeral misconduct, and around $80k in question. I need someone experienced and open to contingency. Please call me at [your number]. Thanks.</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><b><tt>2. Medium (Voicemail or Contact Form)</tt></b><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>Hi, this is [Your Name]. My partner recently passed. We lived together for years and shared finances. I’m now dealing with funeral fraud and $80k in suspicious estate payments.</tt></div>
<div><tt>I need a probate attorney in Broward who handles fraud or contested cases. Contingency or hybrid payment preferred. Please call me back at [Your Number]. Thanks.</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><b><tt>3. Full (Email or Online Inquiry)</tt></b><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>Hello, My partner, Richard, recently passed. We shared a home and assets as life partners. Since his death, I’ve encountered red flags — including a suspicious $80k funeral payment, estate confusion, and possible fraud.</tt></div>
