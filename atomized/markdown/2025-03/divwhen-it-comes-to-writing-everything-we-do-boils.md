## Content
<div>When it comes to writing, everything we do boils down to two things: our audience and why we're writing in the first place.</div>
<div>This week, we're diving into the writing process, which is like your trusty map to becoming a pro-academic writer. It's all about taking it step by step, starting with the prewriting, drafting, and revising phases.</div>
<div>This part is essential because it helps you stay organized and focused on what you want to say. We'll also be talking about some key things to remember while writing, like figuring out your audience, picking the right words (that's what we call diction), nailing down your purpose, and understanding different writing genres.</div>
<div>As you work on your essays throughout the course, we want you to remember these things. Think about how your audience will react to what you're saying, why you're writing in the first place, and how the specific genre of writing you're tackling influences your approach. It's all about making intelligent choices that will make your writing shine and have the impact you want it to have.</div>
