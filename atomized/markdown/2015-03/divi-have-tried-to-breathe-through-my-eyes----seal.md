## Content
<div>I have tried to breathe through my eyes -- seal my nostrils with a pinch, pucker my lips, and just see: Inhaling the pastel arrangements of miscarried  miscalculated perfection of everyday scenery with  voyeuristic displeasure.  I have tried to subvert senses (all of them) to extreme ends where for a few moments reality would begin to die, the body would begin to wither, digits and limbs crawl backwards back into themselves -- petals of a flower at nightfall never to salute the sun again. I have tried. I have. To feel the lack of in a constant circumstance of so much of's.  To turn off the switch that powers simulated environments.  To no longer here the hum we no longer here (but now do).  To no longer stare at illuminated plastic and glass.  To no longer beat percussive thumbs in order to communicate.   To no longer confuse mobile chirps for what once upon a time was birdsong.  To no longer convert. To no longer (un)zip. To no longer load -- down, up, or otherwise. </div>
