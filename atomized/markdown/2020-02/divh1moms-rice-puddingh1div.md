## Content
<div><h1>Mom’s Rice Pudding</h1></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Half gallon whole milk</div>
<div>1 cup of sugar</div>
<div>1 cup of carolina white rice</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Combine and Cook and constantly stir (not with a wooden spoon) on medium heat uncovered. Takes about an hour. </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Take it off the heat</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Add two tablespoons of vanilla extract</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Pour into tin tray </div>
<div>Add raisins </div>
<div>While still hot, sprinkle with cinnamon</div>
<div>Do not cover while still hot </div>
<div>Put in the fridge when it’s cool, covered</div>
<div><br></div>
