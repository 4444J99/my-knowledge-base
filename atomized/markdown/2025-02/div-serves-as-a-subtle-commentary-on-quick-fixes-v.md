<div>📜 2. Every Title Now Has Three Layers</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>📜 Titles will now reflect:</div>
<div>✔ A Meta-Level (course-wide thematic connection).</div>
<div>✔ A Week-Specific Level (literary movement, text, or major theme).</div>
<div>✔ A Literary Technique or Structural Layer (how the week fits into the greater narrative framework).</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Example: Week 6 – Modernism &amp the Fragmentation of Narrative</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>📜 Title: “The Shattered Mirror: Modernism, Self-Perception, &amp the Fractured Mind”</div>
<div>✔ Meta-Level: “The Shattered Mirror” (how narratives no longer reflect reality clearly).</div>
<div>✔ Week-Specific: Modernist literature’s shift toward interiority &amp subjectivity.</div>
<div>✔ Structural Layer: This week represents the “Ordeal” phase in the Hero’s Journey (self-reckoning before transformation).</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>✅ Why This Works:</div>
<div>  •  Ensures that every title communicates multiple layers of meaning.</div>
<div>  •  Allows students to see how literature mirrors itself across different frameworks.</div>
