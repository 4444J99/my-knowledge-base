<div>Day 5: The Power of Storytelling</div>
<div>  •  Concrete: Think of a moment when you had to tell a personal story to someone. How did you decide what details to include or leave out?</div>
<div>  •  Abstract: Why do people tell stories? How do narratives help shape identity and memory?</div>
<div>  •  Connecting: Compare storytelling in spoken vs. written form. How does each medium change the way a story is received?</div>
<div>  •  Exploratory: If you could tell only one story about your life to future generations, which would it be and why?</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Day 6: Painting with Words (Descriptive Writing)</div>
<div>  •  Concrete: Describe a place from your childhood using all five senses—but don’t name it! Can readers guess where it is based on your details?</div>
<div>  •  Abstract: How does descriptive writing influence emotions? Can words alone create an experience as vivid as a photograph or film?</div>
<div>  •  Connecting: Think about a moment in your life that felt like a scene from a movie. How would you describe it to make it just as cinematic on paper?</div>
