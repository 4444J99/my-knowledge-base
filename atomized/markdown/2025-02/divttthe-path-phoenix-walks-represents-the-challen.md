## Content
<div><tt>The path Phoenix walks represents the challenges she has faced in life. She encounters many obstacles, like rough terrain, her own aging body, and even a hunter who disrespects her. Despite this, she keeps going. The story describes her struggle: “Seemingly, the path ran up a hill. She went down slowly, moving from side to side” (Welty, 1941, p.xx).This shows that her journey is not easy, but she never gives up. The path is worn because she has traveled it many times before, just like she has faced struggles throughout her life.</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>Another important symbol is Phoenix’s name. The phoenix is a mythical bird that is reborn from ashes, symbolizing strength and endurance. Even though she is old and forgetful, she keeps pushing forward. At one point, she forgets why she came to town, but she remembers and continues on, showing her deep love for her grandson. Her journey is not for herself but for someone she loves, which shows true sacrifice.</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>Through these symbols, Welty helps us understand Phoenix's perseverance and selflessness. The worn path represents her struggles, and her name reflects her ability to keep going despite difficulties. A Worn Path is a powerful story about love, persistence, and overcoming hardships.</tt></div>
