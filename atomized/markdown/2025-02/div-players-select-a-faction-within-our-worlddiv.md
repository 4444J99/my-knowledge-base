## Content
<div>  •  Players select a faction within our world.</div>
<div>  •  This does not affect their abilities but determines alliances and rivalries.</div>
<div>  •  Example Factions:</div>
<div>  •  The Preservationists – Protect knowledge &amp tradition.</div>
<div>  •  The Reformists – Believe in change and literary evolution.</div>
<div>  •  The Trickster Cult – Seeks disruption &amp subversion.</div>
<div>  •  The Archivists – Devoted to maintaining historical records.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face="ZapfDingbatsITC">✔</font> 3️⃣ Party (Small-Scale Alliances &amp Tactical Play)</div>
<div>  •  Players can form optional parties (2-4 members).</div>
<div>  •  Parties receive minor buffs for working together.</div>
<div>  •  Example Buffs:</div>
<div>  •  Balanced Party (Mix of Archetypes) → +1 Reroll on Random Events.</div>
<div>  •  Pure Party (Same Archetype Group) → Stronger Buffs But Higher Risks.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face="ZapfDingbatsITC">✔</font> 4️⃣ Secret Alignment (Hidden Personal Morality)</div>

<div>  •  They will not know their alignment, but their actions will shift it over time.</div>
