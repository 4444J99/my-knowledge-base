<div>4. The Positive and Negative Pulse (Momentum in Storytelling)</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Stories move through contrast—positive and negative shifts keep a reader engaged.</div>
<div>  •  Not good vs. evil. Instead, think of how momentum changes scene by scene.</div>
<div>  •  A scene should start in one state and end in another.</div>
