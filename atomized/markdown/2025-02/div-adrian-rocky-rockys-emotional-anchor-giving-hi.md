<div>Key Traits:</div>
<div>  •  Represents balance, love, or human connection.</div>
<div>  •  Often sacrificed for the protagonist’s greater goal.</div>
<div>  •  Can serve as a tragic consequence of obsession.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>5. The System (The Uncaring Machine)</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>This is the school, competition, industry, or larger force that the protagonist must navigate. It doesn’t care about individuals—it only values results.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Examples:</div>
<div>  •  Shaffer Conservatory (Whiplash) – A cutthroat world where only perfection matters.</div>
<div>  •  The Boxing Industry (Raging Bull) – A corrupt and punishing system that devours its fighters.</div>
<div>  •  The Feudal Samurai System (Vagabond) – A world where only the strong survive.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Key Traits:</div>
<div>  •  Cold, indifferent, rewards cruelty.</div>
<div>  •  Forces the protagonist to adapt or be crushed.</div>
<div>  •  May represent larger societal expectations.</div>
