<div>Welcome to the Weekly Writing Warm-Up, where you will practice the art of writing. For many of us, the extent of our writing happens in text over our phones, so writing papers and essays feels awkward. To this end, we will participate in Weekly Writing Warm-Ups. These warm-ups are less about what you write and more about putting your fingers on your computer keys. You will not be critiqued on your paragraph or paper structure, but the content does need to be clear. Consistent writing can be a valuable tool to enhance your communication skills, creativity, and self-expression.  Each week, you will be given new instructions (see below) to reflect on and write about. This warm-up activity does not require prework, outlines, or drafts.</div>
