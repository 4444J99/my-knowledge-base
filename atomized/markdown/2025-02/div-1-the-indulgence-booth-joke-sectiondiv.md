## Content
<div>📜 1. The “Indulgence Booth” (Joke Section)</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>📜 Renamed: “The Pardoner’s Purge: Buy Your Way to Redemption (Totally Not a Scam!)”</div>
<div>✔ Students can attempt to “pay” (XP or an assignment task) to have their negative effects removed.</div>
<div>✔ Response: They get a ridiculous, clearly fraudulent reply from the Pardoner character, e.g.:</div>
<div>  •  “Ah yes, dear scholar, for the mere price of 50 XP, your sins are absolved! But wait…your new sin is believing this would actually work.”</div>
<div>  •  “Oh, you wish to remove Writer’s Block? Very well, I shall summon the ghost of Shakespeare! He says… ‘Just write, dummy.’”</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Three-Layered Reference</div>
<div>  1.  Meta-Layer: The absurdity of modern transactional education &amp online paywalls.</div>
<div>  2.  Literary Layer: Ties into Chaucer’s Canterbury Tales and the idea of indulgences.</div>
<div>  3.  Weekly Theme Layer: Will change wording based on the theme (e.g., for Gothic Week, the “Pardoner” might instead be a demonic trickster offering false salvation).</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>✅ Why This Works:</div>
<div>  •  Gives students an in-game joke interaction that reinforces literary themes.</div>
