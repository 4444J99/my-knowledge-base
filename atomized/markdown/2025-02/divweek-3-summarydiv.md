<div>Week 3 | Overview</div>
<div>Drafting and Revision</div>
<div>Now that you've gone through the prewriting, brainstorming, and initial drafting stages, it's time to polish your paper. In this module, we'll equip you with strategies to revise your writing, ensuring it communicates your ideas effectively. </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>&quotRevision is the key to successful writing.&quot (Hofmann, 2017, p. 351)</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Our focus will be on the difference between revision and editing. While editing focuses on grammar, spelling, punctuation, and other surface-level corrections, revision is focused on the larger picture. The goal is to ensure your content, structure, and overall message align with your intended purpose and engage your audience effectively. </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Revision can be challenging but is a crucial phase of good writing. This is your chance to take a step back and critically evaluate your work. You will learn to identify areas that need improvement, such as strengthening your arguments, refining your organization, improving the flow of ideas, and ensuring coherence throughout your writing. As a result, you will enhance the impact of your writing on your readers by giving it a makeover. </div>
