<div>##### **Common Editing Issues to Address:**  </div>
<div>- **Grammar and Sentence Structure:** Run-on sentences, fragments, misplaced modifiers.  </div>
<div>- **Consistency in Tense and Point of View:** Avoid switching perspectives mid-story.  </div>
<div>- **Word Choice:** Replace vague words with precise, impactful vocabulary.  </div>
<div>- **Punctuation:** Ensure proper use of commas, semicolons, and dialogue formatting.  </div>
