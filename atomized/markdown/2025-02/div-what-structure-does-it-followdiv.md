<div>6. Back-and-Forth Brainstorming (Email Process)</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Instead of working alone, let’s email ideas back and forth to refine them.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>How This Works</div>
<div>  1.  You send me an email with:</div>
<div>  •  A rough idea.</div>
<div>  •  A question or problem (e.g., “My character feels flat” or “I don’t know what happens next”).</div>
<div>  2.  I reply with:</div>
<div>  •  Ideas, clarifications, and challenges.</div>
<div>  •  A short writing prompt to spark momentum.</div>
<div>  3.  You respond with a new draft or idea.</div>
<div>  4.  We repeat the cycle until it feels solid.</div>
<div><br></div>
