<div><tt>A revealing moment appears to occur when Phoenix is stopped by a white hunter, who speaks to her in a condescending and patronizing manner. This exchange not only highlights Phoenix's self-assurance despite her circumstances, but also reveals the subtle racism embedded in the story. The hunter assumes she doesn’t know where she’s going simply based on her race, yet Phoenix stands firm in her identity and purpose, demonstrating a quiet resistance against societal expectations.</tt></div>
