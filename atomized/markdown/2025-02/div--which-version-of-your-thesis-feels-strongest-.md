<div>### **IV. Interactive Exercise: “Show, Don’t Tell”**  </div>
<div>#### **1. Purpose**  </div>
<div>- Strengthens **descriptive writing skills** by focusing on **sensory details and imagery**.  </div>
<div>- Helps students **move beyond summarization** to **immersive storytelling**.  </div>
<div>- Encourages students to recognize **the power of vivid descriptions** in making writing more engaging.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>#### **2. Activity: Transforming “Telling” into “Showing”**  </div>
<div>- Students receive **flat, summary-style sentences** and rewrite them **using rich sensory details**.  </div>
<div>- The goal is to **engage the reader** by painting a vivid picture with descriptive language, sensory details, and emotion.  </div>
