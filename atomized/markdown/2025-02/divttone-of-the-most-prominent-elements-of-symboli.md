<div><tt>“Her eyes were blue with age. Her skin had a pattern all its own of numberless branching wrinkles and as though a whole little tree stood in the middle of her forehead...” (The Worn Path E. Welty). This quote illustrates the fragility of her old age –which is in stark contrast to her strength to persist on the worn path. Although her mind may have momentarily forgotten what compelled her to move forward, her love and determination ensured that she achieved her goal. </tt></div>
