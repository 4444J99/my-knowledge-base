<div>Activity: Scene Pulse Mapping</div>
<div>  1.  Write a scene where a character enters a room in one emotional state and leaves in another.</div>
<div>  2.  Identify the shift (positive → negative, or vice versa).</div>
<div>  3.  Rewrite the scene but reverse the emotional change (e.g., if the scene went positive → negative, try negative → positive).</div>

<div>5. The Story Wave (Macro &amp Micro Structure)</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">📈</font> Stories follow a wave pattern—ups and downs in tension and resolution.</div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">📉</font> Each scene also has mini-waves—little rises and falls in intensity.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Activity: Graph Your Story</div>
<div>  1.  Sketch a waveform and mark the high and low points of your story.</div>
<div>  2.  Do the same for a single scene—where does the energy peak and dip?</div>
<div>  3.  Find flat spots and adjust them to add more tension or contrast.</div>
