<div>Understanding Essay Variations</div>
<div>When it comes to writing an essay, there are many different types that one can choose from. Each type has its unique purpose and structure. Understanding the differences between them is vital so that you can write effectively.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Types of expository essays: compare/contrast, descriptive, cause/effect, problem/solution, and process.</div>
<div>One of the most common types of essays is the narrative essay. This type involves telling a story or personal experience. It often includes vivid descriptions, sensory details, and a clear beginning, middle, and end.</div>
