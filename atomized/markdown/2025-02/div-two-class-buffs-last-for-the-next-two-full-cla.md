## Content
<div>  •  Two-Class Buffs: Last for the next two full class sessions.</div>
<div>  •  Three-Class Debuffs: Last for three full class sessions unless countered.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>✔ New Rule: No Passive Buffs Beyond Their Duration</div>
<div>  •  Buffs must be activated within their time frame or they expire.</div>
<div>  •  Cannot be stored indefinitely.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>📜 Why This Works:</div>
<div>✔ Keeps players engaged without overpowered stacking.</div>
<div>✔ Introduces strategy in when buffs are used.</div>
<div>✔ Encourages faster decision-making.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>📜 2. Archetype-Based Item Restrictions</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>📜 Certain items are now locked to specific archetypes.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>✔ Item Categories &amp Restrictions:</div>
<div>  •  Weapons &amp Armor: Only usable by combat-aligned archetypes (e.g., The Warrior).</div>
<div>  •  Literary Relics: Only usable by knowledge-based archetypes (e.g., The Archivist).</div>
<div>  •  Dark Artifacts &amp Cursed Objects: Only usable by morally ambiguous archetypes (e.g., The Fallen Angel).</div>
<div>  •  Healer &amp Support Items: Only usable by support-based archetypes (e.g., The Oracle).</div>
