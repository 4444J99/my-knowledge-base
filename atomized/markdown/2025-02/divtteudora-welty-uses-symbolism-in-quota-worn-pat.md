## Content
<div><tt>Eudora Welty uses symbolism in &quotA Worn Path&quot to convey themes of perseverance and sacrifice, particularly through the character of Phoenix Jackson. As an elderly woman, Phoenix embarks on a long, challenging journey to obtain medication for her sick grandson. Despite the many obstacles she encounters Phoenix continues her journey with unwavering determination. These repeated struggles shows her perseverance and her deep commitment to her loved ones. Her willingness to endure such hardships for the sake of her grandson reflects her selflessness and love, making her a symbol of enduring devotion.</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>One other key symbol that represents perseverance is Phoenix’s “memory.” When Phoenix arrives at the doctor’s office and is asked about her grandson, she initially displays some confusion, reflecting her fading memory. She says, “My grandson. It was my memory had left me. There I sat and forgot why I made my long trip.” Despite the old lady’s aging mind and body, her determination remains strong. This aspect of the story highlights her perseverance, as she is willing to fight through confusion, fatigue, and physical hardship just to ensure that her grandson receives the medicine he needs.</tt></div>
