<div>### **I. Writing Warm-Up**  </div>
<div>#### **1. Purpose**  </div>
<div>- A daily exercise to develop fluency and confidence in writing.  </div>
<div>- Encourages students to explore descriptive writing and sensory details.  </div>
<div>- Helps students refine their ability to &quotshow, not tell&quot in storytelling.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>#### **2. Prompt Options**  </div>
<div>- **Concrete:** Describe your favorite meal using all five senses, but do not name it. Let the reader guess what it is.  </div>
<div>- **Abstract:** What role do vivid descriptions play in the way we remember moments in our lives?  </div>
<div>- **Connecting:** Think about a scene from a book or movie that was especially vivid. How did the description make it feel real to you?  </div>
