<div>SECTION 1: Understanding the Foundations of a Great Story</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Before we analyze specific works or begin crafting ideas, let’s define the core principles that shape a strong narrative.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>1.1 A Story Is About Change Through Conflict</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>At its heart, every story is about a protagonist undergoing transformation due to an antagonistic force rising in their life.</div>
<div>  •  A character must be making choices. Every scene should involve decisions and consequences.</div>
<div>  •  Conflict isn’t just about fights—it’s resistance. This could be an internal struggle, an external rival, or a system that opposes the protagonist.</div>
<div>  •  Antagonism isn’t just the villain. It’s any force that disrupts the protagonist’s normal world and forces them to adapt.</div>
