<div>Week 1: An Introduction to Writing</div>

<div>Day 1: Finding Your Voice in Writing</div>
<div>📌 Instructions: Choose one of the prompts below to explore in depth.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>🔹 Concrete Prompt:</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Describe a moment in your life when clear communication made a significant difference. What was the situation, and how did effective communication impact the outcome?</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>🔹 Abstract Prompt:</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>What does “voice” mean to you beyond just speaking? How do you think your personal voice influences how others perceive you?</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>🔹 Connecting Prompt:</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Consider how your written voice differs from your spoken voice. In what ways do you think writing allows you to communicate differently or more effectively than speaking?</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>🔹 Exploratory Prompt:</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>If you could improve one aspect of your writing immediately, what would it be and why?</div>
