<div>### 4.2 Gothic Conventions  </div>
<div>#### Definition:  </div>
<div>Elements such as dark, eerie settings, supernatural elements, psychological tension, and themes of decay and madness, often used to explore human fears and emotions.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>#### Examples:  </div>
<div>- *The Cask of Amontillado* (Poe) – The catacombs create an oppressive, claustrophobic atmosphere.  </div>
<div>- *Crimson Peak* (film) – A decaying mansion reflects the secrets of its inhabitants.  </div>
<div>- *Rebecca* (Du Maurier) – Manderley, the grand estate, symbolizes mystery and memory.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>#### Discussion Questions  </div>
<div><br></div>
