<div>When writing a narrative essay, you need to think about the basics of narration. The first thing you need to consider is your audience. Who are you writing for? Think about different types of audiences when asking questions like,</div>

<div>Understanding your audience will help you tailor your story and keep them engaged. Additionally, you need to be clear on your purpose. Is there a lesson or moral that you want to convey through your story? Having a clear purpose will give your storytelling more direction and coherence.</div>
