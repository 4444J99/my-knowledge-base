<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">📌</font> 1. Adaptive Structure Per Week</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Each week/module will follow the same format but will have an adjusted number of slides, knowledge checks, and assignments based on Canvas.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Instead of a fixed 17-section model, each week will be structured as follows:</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">📌</font> 1.1 Required Core Sections (Always Included)</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">✅</font> 1️⃣ Overview (Introduction to the week’s topics, themes, and objectives)</div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">✅</font> 2️⃣ Learning Outcomes (Course-level &amp weekly objectives with assessment mapping)</div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">✅</font> 3️⃣ Expectations &amp Readings (Assigned readings, Canvas presentations, and activities for the week)</div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">✅</font> 4️⃣ Major Lecture (Foundations of the Week’s Topic) (The primary instructional content for the week)</div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">✅</font> 5️⃣ Weekly Writing Warm-Up (Assignment with prompt, rubric, and submission details)</div>
