<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">2️⃣</font> Turn It Into an Archetype That Players Can Pick From the Start</div>
<div>  •  The Fallen Angel would be a starting archetype, meaning players enter the game as a rogue figure.</div>
<div>  •  Pros: Brings it in line with the others.</div>
<div>  •  Cons: Loses the transformation element.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">3️⃣</font> Rebrand It as a “Corrupting Influence” That Affects Any Archetype</div>
<div>  •  Instead of being a transformation or archetype, “Fallen Angel” becomes an external effect.</div>
<div>  •  If a player accumulates too many villainous actions, their archetype is permanently altered by a Fallen status.</div>
<div>  •  Pros: Allows flexibility without breaking archetype cohesion.</div>
<div>  •  Cons: Less dramatic than a full transformation.</div>
