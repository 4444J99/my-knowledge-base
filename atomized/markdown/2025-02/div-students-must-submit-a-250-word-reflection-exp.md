<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">📌</font> Weekly Creative Extra Credit Option:</div>

<div>  •  Week 3 (Gothic Horror): Write a 1-page unreliable narrator story.</div>
<div>  •  Week 5 (Modernism): Create a stream-of-consciousness poem.</div>
<div>  •  Week 8 (Postmodernism): Write a fictional encyclopedia entry for a made-up concept.</div>

<div>  •  No pressure—entirely optional.</div>
