# <div>Description serves as a powerful tool in storytelling by immersing readers 

## Context
From document: **ENG 101 - Week 2 Class Schedules (Days 5, 6, 7, & 8)** — chunk 14 (chunk 14/31)

## Content
<div>Description serves as a powerful tool in storytelling by immersing readers in an experience. Good descriptive writing allows the reader to **see, hear, feel, taste, and smell** what is happening in the narrative.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Key benefits of strong description:</div>
<div>- **Enhances engagement**: Draws readers into the scene and makes writing more memorable.</div>
<div>- **Evokes emotion**: Helps readers connect with characters and settings.</div>
<div>- **Strengthens visualization**: Encourages deeper reader immersion in the story.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>##### **Types of Descriptive Techniques**  </div>
<div>1. **Sensory Details** – Engage all five senses to paint a vivid picture.</div>
<div>   - Example: Instead of &quotThe soup was hot,&quot write &quotSteam curled from the bowl as the rich aroma of garlic and herbs filled the air.&quot</div>
<div>2. **Figurative Language** – Use metaphors, similes, and personification.</div>
<div>   - Example: &quotThe sun dipped below the horizon, painting the sky with streaks of crimson and gold.&quot</div>
<div>3. **Precise Word Choice** – Choose specific, evocative words instead of general ones.</div>
<div>   - Example: &quotThe old house creaked and groaned with every gust of wind.&quot</div>
