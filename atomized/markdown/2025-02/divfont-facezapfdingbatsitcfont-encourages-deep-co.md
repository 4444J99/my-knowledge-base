<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">📜</font> 2. Faction Ritual Recovery—Escalating Consequences for Multiple Failures</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face=".AppleColorEmojiUI">📜</font> Factions that fail a ritual can retry the following week, but the penalties grow worse with each failure.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face="ZapfDingbatsITC">✔</font> How Faction Ritual Recovery Works:</div>
<div>  •  A faction that fails a ritual must wait one week before retrying.</div>
<div>  •  Each failure adds an additional penalty, making further attempts riskier.</div>
<div>  •  After three failures, the faction is permanently locked out of rituals.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div><font face="ZapfDingbatsITC">✔</font> Example Scaling Penalties for Repeated Failures:</div>
<div>  •  1st Failure: The faction is barred from making a proposal for one week.</div>
<div>  •  2nd Failure: The faction loses a collective skill bonus (ex: weakened persuasion, limited fate influence).</div>
<div>  •  3rd Failure: The faction is disbanded permanently, forcing its members to scatter or form new alliances.</div>
