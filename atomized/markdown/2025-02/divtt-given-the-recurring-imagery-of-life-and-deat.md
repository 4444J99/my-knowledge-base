<div><tt>In A Worn Path, Eudora Welty uses symbolism to highlight the themes of perseverance and sacrifice. Phoenix Jackson, an elderly Black woman, travels a long and difficult path to get medicine for her sick grandson. Along the way, Welty uses different symbols to show the challenges Phoenix faces and the deep love that motivates her journey.</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>One important symbol in the story is the path itself. The &quotworn path&quot represents Phoenix’s determination and the struggles she has endured overtime. This journey is not new to her—she has walked this same route many times before. Welty writes, &quotThe path ran up a hill,&quot showing how difficult the journey is for Phoenix (Welty). The uphill climb symbolizes the obstacles she faces, not just on this trip but in life. Despite her old age and frailty, she keeps moving forward, demonstrating her resilience and commitment to her grandson. Her repeated travels along this path also suggest the sacrifices she has made over the years, showing how love can push a person beyond their limits.</tt></div>
