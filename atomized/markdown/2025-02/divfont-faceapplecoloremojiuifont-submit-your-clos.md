<div>## **3. Group Assignment: Symbolism Across Cultures**  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>### **Objective**  </div>
<div>Students will **collaborate in small groups** to explore **symbolism in different cultural, historical, or literary contexts** and **present their findings** in an interactive discussion.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>### **Instructions**  </div>
<div>1. **Form Groups (3-4 students each).**  </div>
<div>2. **Select a Theme:** Choose a **symbol or metaphor** from one of the following areas:  </div>
<div>   - **Mythology &amp Folklore** (e.g., the Phoenix, the Ouroboros, the Tree of Life).  </div>
<div>   - **Social Justice Movements** (e.g., the raised fist, the pink triangle, the broken chain).  </div>
<div>   - **Religious or Philosophical Symbolism** (e.g., the lotus, the cross, the yin-yang).  </div>
