<div>### **I. Writing Warm-Up**  </div>
<div>#### **1. Purpose**  </div>
<div>- Reinforces fluency in writing by encouraging free expression.  </div>
<div>- Prepares students for drafting their narrative essays by focusing on structure and storytelling techniques.  </div>
<div>- Encourages confidence in developing a complete story arc.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>#### **2. Prompt Options**  </div>
<div>- **Concrete:** Think of a time when you had to make a difficult decision. How did you approach the situation, and what was the outcome?  </div>
<div>- **Abstract:** What makes a story compelling? What elements do the best stories have in common?  </div>
<div>- **Connecting:** How does writing a narrative differ from telling one aloud? What are the advantages and challenges of each?  </div>
<div>- **Exploratory:** If you could receive feedback on one aspect of your storytelling, what would it be and why?  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>#### **3. Discussion After Writing**  </div>
<div>- What was challenging about today’s warm-up?  </div>
<div>- Did you find yourself focusing more on **plot, character, or emotion**?  </div>
