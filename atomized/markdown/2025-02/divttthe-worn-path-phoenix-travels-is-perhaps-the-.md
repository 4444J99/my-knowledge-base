The path is rough, filled with obstacles, and requires great strength to navigate, just as Phoenix’s life has been full of struggles and hardships. Despite the challenges, Phoenix persists, making her way forward step by step. This path is symbolic of her unwavering commitment to her grandson’s well-being, even when faced with physical limitations and environmental challenges. Phoenix’s journey along the path highlights the theme of perseverance, as she continues forward without hesitation, driven by love and duty.</tt></div>
<div><tt>Another significant symbol is the encounter with the pheasant, which Phoenix chases during her journey. The pheasant represents both a distraction and a test of Phoenix’s resolve. The pheasant, in this instance, can be seen as a metaphor for the challenges and temptations that may divert someone from their goals.
