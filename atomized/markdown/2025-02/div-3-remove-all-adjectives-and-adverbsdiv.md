<div>C. The “What If?” Chain</div>
<div>  •  Start with a single “What if?” question (e.g., “What if a man wakes up and no one remembers him?”).</div>
<div>  •  Answer it, then ask another “What if?” based on that answer.</div>
<div>  •  Keep going until a full story takes shape.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>5. Final Takeaways</div>
<div>  •  Study what inspires you – Reverse-engineer stories he loves.</div>
<div>  •  Don’t fear the vomit draft – No one starts with perfection.</div>
<div>  •  Play with different structures – Different stories need different shapes.</div>
<div>  •  Write fast, edit slow – Get ideas down first, then refine.</div>
