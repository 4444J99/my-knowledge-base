<div>Step 1: Write Your Logline</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>A logline is a one-sentence summary of your story that captures its core conflict.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Here are five real logline examples from film and manga:</div>
<div>  1.  A fugitive samurai roams Japan seeking the true meaning of strength, only to realize his greatest enemy is himself. (Vagabond)</div>
<div>  2.  A young jazz drummer joins an elite music program, only to be pushed beyond his limits by an abusive mentor. (Whiplash)</div>
<div>  3.  A boy bullies a deaf girl in school, then years later tries to make amends for his past cruelty. (A Silent Voice)</div>
<div>  4.  A troubled teen discovers a passion for painting, but finds that the pursuit of art demands everything from him. (Blue Period)</div>
