<div>D. The Kishōtenketsu (Four-Act Structure)</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Best for: Non-Western storytelling, slice-of-life, emotional narratives.</div>
<div>  1.  Ki (Introduction) – Establish characters and setting.</div>
<div>  2.  Shō (Development) – Expand on the world, introduce small events.</div>
