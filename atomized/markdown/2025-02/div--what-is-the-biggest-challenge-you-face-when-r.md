<div>### **II. Lecture: The Art of Revision &amp Self-Editing**  </div>
<div>#### **1. Purpose**  </div>
<div>- Helps students develop a systematic approach to **editing and revising** their writing.  </div>
<div>- Demonstrates techniques for identifying and fixing common writing issues.  </div>
<div>- Encourages students to critically analyze their own work before final submission.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>#### **2. Revision vs. Editing: What’s the Difference?**  </div>
<div>- **Revision:** Focuses on **big-picture changes**—structure, flow, clarity, and engagement.  </div>
<div>- **Editing:** Focuses on **surface-level changes**—grammar, punctuation, and word choice.  </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>##### **Key Revision Strategies:**  </div>
<div>1. **Read Aloud Test** – Hearing your own words helps identify awkward phrasing.  </div>
<div>2. **Reverse Outlining** – Outline your draft after writing to check structure and organization.  </div>
