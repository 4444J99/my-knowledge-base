<div>The water went hot and I carefully pulled each blade free from the knife block and placed them in the sink. The steak knifes, the vegetable knifes, the parting knifes, the coming knifes, the going knifes. They all blended together with the basin of the sink. Metallic. The handles stood out. </div>
<div><br></div>

<div><br></div>

<div><br></div>
<div>Knifes are funny--they take on the form of whatever it is around them. They can be cold or they could be warm--they could be necessary, or they could be necessary. Playful or playful. </div>
