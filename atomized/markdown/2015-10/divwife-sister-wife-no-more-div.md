## Content
<div>Wife. Sister. Wife no more. </div>
<div>So now disguised in nothing but friend's clothing, I crossed my legs sitting just to her left, and chewed the fat of every gossip she needed lay waste. </div>
<div>I cracked my neck in certainty, yipping yapping:</div>
<div>&quotAsk him. For all of those things you demand.  Next time he is here, sudden and quick behind your naked back, surprised as he bites your ear--ask him: </div>
<div>come in vigor and in power. </div>
<div>Come in power and in vigor. </div>
<div>In definition. In aim. In purpose</div>
<div>Ask him, and he'll come. </div>
<div>In every way you need him to.&quot</div>
