# <div>Why do you always come at night?</div>

## Context
From document: Semele — chunk 2 (chunk 2/3)

## Content
<div>Why do you always come at night?</div>
<div>In the deepest darker depths of night, silent </div>
<div>And hiding, under cunning disguise: you come, you go.  Your </div>
<div>shame soaks my sheets so deep, your flame burns quiet,</div>
<div>But Oh, how it burns.  Internal. External. Eternal. </div>
<div>I need you to come to me without jealousy.  I need you to come to me without her eyes. Her eyes. </div>
<div>Her eyes. </div>
<div>They follow you. I feel them falling heavy through every window frame, bursting busting behind every door. I feel them against my back when I ride the thunder that is your thighs. </div>
<div>You hide from her in me. </div>
<div>You hide. You hide. </div>
<div>Come to me like you come to her. </div>
<div>In vigor and in power. In power and in vigor. </div>
<div>In definition. In aim. In purpose. </div>
<div>Come to me like you come to her. </div>
<div>Come to her like you once upon a time would come to me. </div>
<div>Never me, anymore. </div>
<div>Never me. Nor ever was. </div>
<div>She never sat where I could sit. </div>
<div>Never did. Never could. Next to him.  </div>
<div>Never burdened nor buried by any of his disasters. </div>
<div>Never was. Never will be. Not for him. </div>
