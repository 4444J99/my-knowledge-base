## Content
<div>The Auto-Rev-Epistemic-Engine (v4.2) is a production-ready, self-governing orchestration framework that seamlessly integrates eight phases (ingestion → finalization) with human oversight at four critical gates. It strikes a harmonious balance between automation and governance through the use of Human Resource Groups (HRGs), while also providing clear Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and escalation mechanisms. Additionally, it optimizes resource utilization through Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) (utilization tracking and waste governance), ensures reproducibility by pinning models, seeds, and maintaining an immutable state, and incorporates ethics and reflexivity through axioms, normative audits, and meta-commentary. Furthermore, it offers complete auditability through append-only logs, BLAKE3 hashing, and compliance attestations.</div>
