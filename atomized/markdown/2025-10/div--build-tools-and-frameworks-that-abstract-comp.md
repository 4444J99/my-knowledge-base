<div>## 3. **Schema.org** – Interoperability, Clarity &amp Stability</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>### Original Intention &amp Purpose</div>
<div>Schema.org was created as a collaborative initiative (supported by Google, Microsoft, Yahoo, Yandex) to establish a shared vocabulary for structured data. Its goal is to make digital content universally understandable across different platforms, search engines, and applications[[1]](https://schema.org/).</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>### Core Mission</div>
<div>Create, maintain, and promote standardized schemas that enable machines to accurately comprehend content across the web—improving discoverability, interoperability, and user experiences.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>### Guiding Principles</div>
<div>- **Interoperability**: A universal schema prevents fragmentation and ensures data works across multiple platforms and applications</div>
