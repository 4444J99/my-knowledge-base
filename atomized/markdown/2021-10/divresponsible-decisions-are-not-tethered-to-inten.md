## Content
<div>Responsible decisions are not tethered to intent; in fact, they are entirely independent of them; regardless of intentions, an accident, or the diversion to mistake, affects others both within and outside an individual’s network, directly and indirectly. I am mindful that all individuals play an enormous role as a cog in the machinations of society; my actions will be made with careful consideration through a more mature and responsible lens. I accept my responsibility for endangering others while in possession of a vehicle. I commit to passing on what I learned as a result of my mistakes; by changing my negative habits and behaviors, I can also help to influence family and friends in my circle. I am grateful for the time I have to reflect on these matters. I often spend a lot of time thinking about the ripple of consequences, both caused and avoided. </div>
