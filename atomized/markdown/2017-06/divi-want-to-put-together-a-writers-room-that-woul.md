## Content
<div>I want to put together a writer’s room that would take place over the course of ~12 sessions (not necessarily weekly). The goal is to create a seasons worth of scripts for a show (the length yet to be decided upon). The theoretical show might be a 10 week season, each episode might be 20 minutes, or 50-60 minutes long (1 page is equal to roughly one minute of screen time). This all depends on what we feel would be the right length for what I’m pitching below. What I want this 12 session writer’s room to accomplish is to put together an entire season arc , character sketches, fully written episodes, future prospects of future seasons, as well as a pitch ready, fully deliverable concept to a network like Netflix or HBO.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>A session will be around 1-2 hours and would be a place for us all to just bang out a ton of ideas, take notes, and do basic sketching. Involvement comes in multiple levels and is not all or nothing: you can just be an idea person, or you can potentially helm an entire episode yourself at some point . There will be a google drive folder, so for those who can’t make a meeting you will be invited to still collaborate online.</div>
