## Context

## Content
<div>The team of writer’s will all receive credit on the concept of the season as a whole. The complete written episodes will be credited to the actual person putting pen to paper (or finger to key) as ‘Written by.’</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>I will be the head writer; what this means is that I will be running the theoretical show—all ideas pass through me—and I will be credited as ‘Created by.’ I intend on writing a majority of the episodes myself in order to maintain a cohesive voice/tone. </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>But I need you guys! There is no way a show as complicated as LOST could have been written by one person, and while I might be doing the actual writing myself, the mastermind group will be the wood the fuels the creative fire. </div>
