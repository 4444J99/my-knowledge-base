## Content
<div>Grandpa Pepe’s Thanksgiving Stuffing</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>3 lbs of sausage meat with fennel seeds. If cand find add fennel as u cook</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>3 bags pepperidge farm cubed stuffing</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>3 1/2 lbs chopped sweet onions </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>2 celery stoukes </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>2 lbs mushrooms</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Open the stuffing big bowl. </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Add 2 32 ounce chicken broth. Mix well</div>
<div>In big fry pan cook the sausage. As u cook use spatula to chop the meat so dont stick</div>
<div>Take meat when cooked add to stuffing mix</div>
<div>Fry onions and chopped celery til soft add that </div>
<div>Cop mushrooms to 1 to 1 1/2 inch pieces fry that till cooked add to mixture</div>
<div>Mix well</div>
<div>Add about 1 cup locatelli cheese mix well</div>
<div>Dont add salt sausage very salty</div>
<div>You can add 4 eggs in morn before you cook. Holds together better </div>
<div>Dont have to though. </div>
