# <div>Dynamic Content Generation:</div>

## Context
From document: This application, "Infinite Wiki," is a dynamic, AI-powered hypertext… — chunk 2 (chunk 2/11)

## Content
<div>Dynamic Content Generation:</div>
<div>Text Definitions: The app uses the gemini-2.5-flash-lite model for its speed, streaming a single-paragraph definition for the chosen topic. The content appears character-by-character with a blinking cursor, providing immediate feedback to the user.</div>
<div>Generative ASCII Art: Simultaneously, the app calls the gemini-2.5-flash model with a sophisticated prompt to generate a unique piece of ASCII art that visually represents the topic's essence. This art is also streamed onto the page with a &quottyping&quot effect, adding a distinct aesthetic flair.</div>
<div>User Interface &amp Experience:</div>
<div>Minimalist Design: The UI is clean, text-focused, and styled like a modern, readable document, using a monospaced font for the body and a monospace font for the art.</div>
<div>Loading States: The app provides clear feedback during API calls. While waiting for content to start streaming, a skeleton loader is shown. While content is arriving, it's displayed in a non-interactive streaming mode.</div>
<div>Interactivity: Once content has fully loaded, it becomes interactive, with hover effects on words to indicate they are clickable.</div>
