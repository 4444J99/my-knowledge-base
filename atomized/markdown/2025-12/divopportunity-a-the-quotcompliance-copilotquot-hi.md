<div>Opportunity A: The &quotCompliance Copilot&quot (Highest Value)</div>
<div> * The Gap: No major tool currently automates the specific compliance checklists for NY's &quotGood Cause Eviction&quot or NJ's Lead Paint laws.</div>
<div> * The Solution: A mobile-first app that serves as a &quotCompliance Shield.&quot</div>
<div>   * Feature: Good Cause Calculator: Input a unit's rent and CPI data; the app tells the agent the exact maximum legal rent increase for renewal.</div>
<div>   * Feature: Inspection Tracker: Automated alerts for NJ landlords/agents when lead paint certifications are expiring.</div>
<div>   * Why it wins: It protects agents from lawsuits. Fear is a powerful sales driver.</div>
<div>Opportunity B: The &quotAlgorithmic Pricing&quot Alternative</div>
<div> * The Gap: In late 2024/2025, NY bans on &quotalgorithmic price-fixing&quot software (like RealPage) have left landlords/agents without pricing guidance. They can no longer use shared private data to set rents.</div>
<div> * The Solution: A &quotClean Data&quot Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) tool.</div>
<div>   * Feature: A rental pricing engine that explicitly uses only public, aggregated data (compliant with new antitrust laws) to suggest optimal rent prices.</div>
