## Content
<div>Hypertext Navigation: The AI as a Connector</div>
<div>This remains the core mechanic, but with a twist. When you click on a word in one of your notes:</div>
<div>If a note for that word exists: You are taken directly to your existing note, just like a standard wiki link.</div>
<div>If no note exists: This is where the magic happens. The AI doesn't just give you a generic definition. It performs several actions:</div>
<div>Semantic Search: It searches your entire knowledge base for passages, sentences, or other notes that are conceptually related to the clicked word.</div>
<div>Dynamic Synthesis: It generates a new, temporary &quotsynthesis page&quot that summarizes how this new concept relates to your existing knowledge. It might say, &quotYou haven't written about 'Quantum Entanglement' directly, but it relates to your notes on 'Philosophy of Time' and your project brief for 'Project Stardust'. Here are the relevant excerpts...&quot</div>
<div>Prompted Creation: This synthesis page would end with a prompt: &quotWould you like to create a permanent note for 'Quantum Entanglement'?&quot This turns passive exploration into an active process of knowledge creation.</div>
<div>The &quotRandom&quot Button Becomes &quotResurface&quot</div>
