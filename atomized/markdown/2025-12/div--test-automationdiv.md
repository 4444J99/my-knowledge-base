<div>### **4. Security and Compliance**</div>
<div>Proactively address vulnerabilities and compliance requirements:</div>
<div>- [ ] **Authentication and authorization**:</div>
<div>  - Use OAuth 2.0, SSO, or OpenID Connect for consistent authentication.</div>
<div>  - Enforce RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) and least privilege principles.</div>
<div>- [ ] **Data protection**:</div>
<div>  - Encrypt data at rest (e.g., AES-256) and in transit (e.g., TLS 1.2+).</div>
<div>  - Use secrets management tools like HashiCorp Vault.</div>
<div>- [ ] **Compliance**:</div>
<div>  - Ensure adherence to regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, or SOC 2.</div>
<div>  - Conduct audits and maintain logs for traceability.</div>
<div>- [ ] **Threat modeling**:</div>
<div>  - Identify potential attack vectors and mitigate them.</div>
