<div>🌊 EMOTIONAL WAVE PROFILE</div>
<div>There are 3 primary wave types—yours is tied to the Solar Plexus Center, and likely expresses as a &quotbuild and crash&quot wave (from Channel 59–6 + Gate 36):</div>
<div>Stage</div>
<div>Description</div>
<div>Your Experience</div>
<div>High Crest</div>
<div>Excited, passionate, optimistic</div>
<div>“This is it! Let’s go now!”</div>
<div>Dip</div>
<div>Moody, uncertain, withdrawn</div>
<div>“I’m unsure. Maybe this is all wrong.”</div>
<div>Neutral Clarity</div>
<div>Balanced, uncharged, calm awareness</div>
<div>“It’s neither good nor bad. It just is.” ✅ Decision point🔁 TIMING RULE:</div>
<div>Never decide in the crest or dip. Wait for the neutral.</div>
<div>This applies to:</div>
<div>  •  Creative project launches</div>
<div>  •  Commitments or collaborations</div>
<div>  •  Spending money</div>
<div>  •  Saying yes to opportunities</div>
<div>  •  Declaring ideas “finished”</div>
<div>🧰 WORKFLOW INTEGRATION</div>
<div>🌒 DAILY SYSTEM ALIGNMENT:</div>
<div>Morning / Early Wave  •  Ideal for generating ideas  •  Not for choosing between them</div>
<div>  •  Free-write, sketch, riff</div>
<div>Afternoon / Mid-Wave  •  Might feel moody or flat  •  Rest, archive, light structure</div>
