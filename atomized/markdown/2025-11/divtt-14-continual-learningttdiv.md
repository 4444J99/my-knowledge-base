<div><tt>### **14. Continual Learning**</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>**Hybrid Approach (6 methods integrated):**</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>1. **Replay**: Episodic memory buffer  (1-2% of data) </tt></div>
<div><tt>1. **Parameter Regularization**: EWC-style importance weighting  </tt></div>
<div><tt>1. **Functional Regularization**: Knowledge distillation on anchor points  </tt></div>
<div><tt>1. **Optimization**: Sharpness-aware minimization (flat minima)  </tt></div>
<div><tt>1. **Context-Dependent**: Task-specific adapters with gating  </tt></div>
<div><tt>1. **Template-Based**: Prototypical networks for class-incremental learning  </tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>**Catastrophic Forgetting Prevention:**</tt></div>
<div><tt><br></tt></div>
<div><tt>- Backward transfer: Track performance on old tasks </tt></div>
<div><tt>- Target: &lt5% accuracy drop on Task₁ after learning Task₁₀</tt></div>
