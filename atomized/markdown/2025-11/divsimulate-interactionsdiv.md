<div>Seed World defines the initial metaformeme cluster, mythic framing, and wave parameters for a new world. Inputs: a short concept brief, intended media types, constraints from VISION and SYSTEM layers. Output: a WORLD-MYTH-SPEC artifact with core vocabulary and values.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Expand Entities grows characters, locations, timelines, and operational structures consistent with the seed. Inputs: seed spec, desired granularity, and target media. Output: WORLD-MYTH-INDEX-CHAR and related artifacts populating characters and relations.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Simulate Interactions runs narrative or operational scenarios within the world: story episodes, user journeys, or project sprints. It is governed by wave parameters and can be either speculative (prototyping) or reflective (replaying actual events).</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Archive Deltas summarizes what changed during simulation, writing WORLD and STORY artifacts into $ARCHIVE_ROOT/worlds and $ARCHIVE_ROOT/characters. It also updates continuity indices.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Emit Next-Run Prompts constructs SEAL-aligned prompts for future AIs, instructing them how to continue waves, explore branches, or refactor material. These prompts are stored in $PROMPT_ROOT and referenced in $ARCHIVE_ROOT/meta.</div>
