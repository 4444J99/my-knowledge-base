## Content
<div>Your knowledge bases contain:</div>
<div>- Brainstorms that never made it to code</div>
<div>- Refined ideas across different AI conversations</div>
<div>- Evolution of concepts through multiple LLM sessions</div>
<div>- Context that exists ONLY in conversational memory</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>**The Problem:** This layer has no version control, no git history, no persistence beyond chat logs.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>### **2. The Gaps Between Layers**</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>**AI Brains → Personal Repos:**</div>
<div>- Manual copy/paste from conversations</div>
<div>- Lost context between sessions</div>
<div>- No automated way to extract &quotthe good stuff&quot</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>**Personal Repos → Org Repos:**</div>
<div>- Which personal repos fed into which org repos?</div>
<div>- What was the decision logic for promotion?</div>
<div>- How do you track lineage?</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>**Org Repos ↔ AI Brains:**</div>
<div>- Your AI conversations now reference your code</div>
<div>- But there's no feedback loop to capture new insights back into repos</div>
