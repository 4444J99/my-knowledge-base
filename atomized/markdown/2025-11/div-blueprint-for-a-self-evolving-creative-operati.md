<div>## Conceptual Layers of the Creative OS (Vision, System, Continuity, Handoff)</div>
<div>**Vision Layer:** The vision layer defines the philosophical and imaginative foundations of the creative OS. It establishes *why* this system exists and articulates the meta-principles guiding its evolution. For example, a new concept might be named as a fusion of existing memetic roots, so that each iteration of naming builds upon previous context (a “meta-formeme” approach).
