## Content
<div>A simple script that:</div>
<div>1. Scans all your personal repos</div>
<div>2. Checks: Is it a fork? Have you modified it? When was last commit?</div>
<div>3. Generates a triage report with recommendations</div>
<div>4. Creates a decision matrix for you to review</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>**This will give you concrete data on what you actually have, not what you think you have.**</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>---</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>## **THE BRUTAL TRUTH**</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>You don't need a four-layer integration architecture **yet**.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>You need:</div>
<div>1. **Inventory** - What do you actually have?</div>
<div>2. **Triage** - What's valuable vs. noise?</div>
<div>3. **Cleanup** - Remove redundancy and chaos</div>
<div>4. **Foundation** - Build the decision framework</div>
<div>5. **Then** build the synthesis system</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>**You're trying to organize a library before knowing what books are on the floor.**</div>
<div><br></div>

<div><br></div>
