<div>She could kill them by smiling; by simply retracting each end of her lips, bearing just slightly her upper teeth that all played together quite nice, that sat perfectly upon her bottom lip. With just a half hearted smile she'd swallow a heart whole, and she smiled every where she roamed, unknowingly stealing the pieces of men they locked tight inside underneath their thick iron chests.  These pieces collected in an ever expanding net and dragged behind her--but she could not see them, she could not feel the weight of them--not until all these tiny pieces gathered in such a way that caused a new being to spring forth from the amassed mass.  A man, naked--broke free from the net. Tapping her on the shoulder, she turned, armed with the smile. He picked up all her teeth from the concrete, but left her face face down. </div>
