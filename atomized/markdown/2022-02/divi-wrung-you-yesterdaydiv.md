## Content
<div>I wrung you yesterday,</div>
<div>some million-odd years ago.</div>
<div>Your answer familiar;</div>
<div>my question, also so.</div>
<div>I miss your snide remarks,</div>
<div>your crooked lower tooth.</div>
<div>You miss?--</div>
<div>Nothing. No one. </div>
<div>Never your mind worry.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>A lesson taught, never</div>
<div>learned. Stickiest ideas oft</div>
<div>offer no thing crueler</div>
<div>than Severest winters--</div>
<div>Yet, how quick I am, grab</div>
<div>My scarf, my coat, how quick</div>
<div>I ignore a sun-blessed </div>
<div>Reflection:</div>
<div>Step</div>
<div>Time</div>
<div>Step,</div>
<div>I</div>
<div>walk</div>
<div>in thru</div>
<div>the past. </div>
