<div>Ingredients </div>

<div>4-5 cloves crushed garlic</div>
<div>Extra virgin olive oil </div>
<div>5 fresh basil leaves </div>
<div>Parsley </div>
<div>Black ground pepper </div>
<div>Garlic powder</div>
<div>1 piece of pork for sauce</div>
<div>1 piece of beef for sauce</div>
<div>1 pork braciole</div>
<div>1 pack of sweet or hot sausage </div>
<div>You can use pork Ribs if you like too</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>In a sauce pan sauté garlic in EVO until almost golden</div>
<div>Add meat - stirring meat till all sides are browned. (Stirring almost constantly) </div>
<div>Add cans of sauce</div>
<div>Keep on med flame until boil</div>
<div>Lower flame and add seasoning. </div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Do not use a tin stock pot!!!! Meat will burn and stick.</div>
